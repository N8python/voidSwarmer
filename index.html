Instructions:

Press w/up arrow to boost towards the mouse pointer.

Click to shoot a stream of bullets.

Kill all the enemies to pass a level.

Be wary, most enemies can exert gravitational force on you.

Buy upgrades in the shop. You can choose between three different traits to upgrade.

The first trait you upgrade will be your primary trait. The next, your secondary. And the final one, your tertiary.

Primary traits go to level 10, secondary traits go to level 5, and tertiary traits go to level 3.

Good luck!
